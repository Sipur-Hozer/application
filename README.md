# Sipor Hozer Application 

Sipor Hozer, a social bookstore chain, trains individuals facing barriers to employment. Through its program, these individuals acquire professional and social development tools.
Our application's main goal is to create a comfortable and intuitive interface that helps them achieve these objectives by offering the following services:

## For Employees:

1. **Personal account** in the system with the following information:
     1. full name
     2. date of birth
     3. Shift history
     4. personal tasks/goals

2. **clocking in and out of shifts** - Requires them to fill in location, type of job, and information adjusted according to the specific job.
3. **filling their cleaning shifts** - Requires them to fill their assigned duties and upload a photo of the finished task.
4. **updating their personal progress according to their predefined goals*** (optional)

## For Manager:

1. **List of their employees**
2. **Adding new employee** (to the list)
3. **Personal details about their employees** - as detailed above
4. **Assigning cleaning duties and shifts**
5. **Generating employee reports** - provides personal information about each employee's work, progress, and salary during the month.
